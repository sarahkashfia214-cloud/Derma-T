Our purpose

Derma‑T is a student‑led nonprofit advancing skin health education, hygiene, and dermatology awareness — on campus and beyond Kingston.

Education

Evidence‑based workshops & guest speakers (dermatologists, med students).

Outreach

Hygiene kits, skin health campaigns, community partnerships.

Awareness

Debunk myths, promote sun protection & inclusive skin health.
