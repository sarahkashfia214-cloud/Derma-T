DERMA-T

STUDENT-LED NON-PROFIT

Derma-T is a student-led nonprofit project dedicated to promoting education around skin health, hygiene, and awareness. Our goal is to provide students with insight into dermatology, connect with healthcare professionals, and positively impact the community.

Derma-T

Our Mission

We aim to make dermatology education more accessible by raising awareness about skin health, hygiene, and common dermatological conditions through student-led learning, outreach, and community initiatives.

What We Do

Skin Education

We share easy-to-understand information about skin health and hygiene.

Community Outreach

We create awareness campaigns and service initiatives for the community.

Healthcare Connection

We help students learn from healthcare professionals in dermatology.
