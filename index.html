About Derma-T

Derma-T is a student-led nonprofit project dedicated to promoting education around skin health, hygiene, and awareness.

Our goal is to help students learn more about dermatology, connect with healthcare professionals, and create meaningful community initiatives through education, service, and fundraising.

What We Focus On

  • Skin health education
  • Hygiene kit initiatives
  • Dermatology awareness campaigns
  • Student and healthcare professional engagement

Our Mission

Making dermatology education more approachable.

We aim to provide students with insight into dermatology while also creating opportunities to positively impact the community through educational, service, and financial initiatives.

What We Do

Our projects are designed to be educational, practical, and community-focused.

Educational Posts

Creating simple and engaging skin health information for students and the wider community.

Hygiene Kits

Supporting outreach through hygiene-focused care packages and community service initiatives.

Professional Talks

Helping students connect with healthcare professionals and learn about dermatology pathways.
